# Account and Sign-In

## Create an account

1. Go to the sign-up page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Confirm your email if verification is required.

## Sign in

1. Go to the sign-in page.
2. Enter your credentials.
3. Complete any required verification steps.

## Sign out

Use the user menu in the top navigation and select sign out.

## Access problems

- If you receive an unauthorized message, contact your tenant admin.
- If sign-in fails repeatedly, reset your password and retry.
